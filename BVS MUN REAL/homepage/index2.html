A Message From The Secretary General

Precious Rajyalaxmi Shah

Secretary General

Dear students, parents, and future diplomats, It gives me great pleasure to extend a warm invitation to the Brihaspati Vidyasadan Model United Nations, set to take place on August 23rd, 24th, and 25th, 2024. I am thrilled to witness your enthusiastic participation in debates that will explore solutions to both global and local issues. Working alongside my dedicated team, we have crafted an MUN experience unlike any other. BVSMUN promises not only academic enrichment but also the opportunity to forge new connections and create lasting memories. At BVSMUN, every delegate will find a supportive environment to participate, speak, and learn. Our aim is to empower delegates to step out of their comfort zones, master the art of public speaking, and hone diplomatic skills. With eight committees spanning the realms of history, politics, and crime, delegates will embark on a journey of discovery and growth. Just as the Model United Nations has inspired me, I am confident that BVSMUN 2024 will instill confidence and inspiration in each participant. I am excited to embark on this journey with you and extend a heartfelt welcome to BVSMUN 2024 this August!

Warm regards,

Precious Rajyalaxmi Shah,

Secretary General
